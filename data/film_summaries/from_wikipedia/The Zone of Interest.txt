The Zone of Interest is an upcoming American-British-Polish drama film, written and directed by Jonathan Glazer, loosely based on novel of the same name by Martin Amis. It was shot in 2021 in Auschwitz.It will have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in May 2023.

=== Development ===
Glazer confirmed development of the project in 2019. Shooting started in June 2021 and reportedly wrapped up in January 2022
