The New Boy is an upcoming 2023 Australian drama film written and directed by Warwick Thornton. It stars Cate Blanchett, Aswan Reid, Deborah Mailman and Wayne Blair. Blanchett also serves as a producer under her Dirty Films banner.
It is scheduled to have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in May 2023 in Un Certain Regard.

== Production ==
In February 2022, Cate Blanchett joined the cast of the film, with Warwick Thornton directing from a screenplay he wrote, with Blanchett serving as a producer under her Dirty Films banner. In December 2022, Aswan Reid, Deborah Mailman, Wayne Blair, Shane Brady, Tyrique Brady, Laiken Woolmington, Kailem Miller, Kyle Miller, Tyzailin Roderick and Tyler Spencer joined the cast of the film.Principal photography concluded by December 2022.


== Release ==
It will have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in Un Certain Regard.
