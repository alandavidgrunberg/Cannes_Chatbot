Anatomy of a Fall (French: Anatomie d'une chute) is an upcoming French thriller film directed by Justine Triet from a screenplay co-written by Triet and Arthur Harari. It stars Sandra Hüller as a writer trying to prove her innocence in her husband's death. The film will have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in May 2023  where it will compete for the Palme d'Or, and will be released theatrically in France by Le Pacte on 23 August 2023.


== Plot ==
Sandra, a German writer, is arrested for murder following her husband's death in the snow under mysterious circumstances and tries to prove her innocence during the trial.

== Production ==
On 10 July 2021, Variety reported that Les Films Pelleas and Les Films de Pierre would co-produce Justine Triet's fourth feature film, Anatomie d'une chute, co-written by Triet and Arthur Harari. Described as "a Hitchcockian procedural thriller", the film marks Triet's second collaboration with Sandra Hüller following the 2019 film Sibyl.Principal photography began at the end of February 2022 and wrapped on 13 May 2022. Filming took place mostly in the region of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, such as in Maurienne and Villarembert in Savoie, and in Montbonnot-Saint-Martin and Grenoble in Isère. It was also shot in Charente-Maritime and in Paris.


== Release ==
The film will have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in May 2023. Le Pacte will release it theatrically in France on 23 August 2023.
