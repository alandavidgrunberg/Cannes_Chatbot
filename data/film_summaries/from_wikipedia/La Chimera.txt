La Chimera is an upcoming Italian-French-Swiss drama film directed by Alice Rohrwacher and starring Josh O'Connor and Isabella Rossellini.


== Cast ==
Josh O'Connor as Arthur
Isabella Rossellini
Carol Duarte
Alba Rohrwacher
Vincenzo Nemolato


== Production ==
Filming began in Tuscany in February 2022.In May 2022, it was announced that Neon acquired North American distribution rights to the film.


== Release ==
It will have its world premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in May 2023.
