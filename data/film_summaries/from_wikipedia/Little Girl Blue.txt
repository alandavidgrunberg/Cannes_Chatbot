Little Girl Blue is an upcoming French-Belgian docudrama film written and directed by Mona Achache about her mother's life, starring Marion Cotillard as Achache's mother. It will premiere at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in the Special Screenings section.

== Production ==
On 7 January 2020, it was announced that France's CNC had granted an advance on earnings to Mona Achache's documentary project Little Girl Blue, whose production details were yet to be formalized.On 10 December 2022, it was announced that Achache had recently filmed a docudrama about her mother's life starring Marion Cotillard as the director's mother and that Achache would also star in the film. The editing process started on 2 January 2023.The film is a co-production between France's Les Films du Poisson with France 2 and Belgium's Wrong Men and RTBF. Tandem will distribute it in France. Sales are handled by Paris-based company Charades.


=== Filming ===
Filming took place in Mulhouse in the Grand Est region of France between 20 November and 10 December 2022.


== Marketing ==
The first poster for the film was unveiled on 24 April 2023.
