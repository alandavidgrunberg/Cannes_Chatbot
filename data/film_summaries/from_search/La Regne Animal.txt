Animal Kingdom, directed by Thomas Cailley, will open the Un Certain Regard selection at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival. This science fiction, adventure and drama film stars Romain Duris, Adèle Exarchopoulos and Nathalie Richard in a world where man mutates into animals.

Two years after the first mutations from human to animal, society tries to adapt to this new reality. Specialized centers are created to take care of and treat these "creatures". However, a convoy accident causes the creatures to disperse into the wild, leading to a series of unexpected events.

Director Thomas Cailley works with screenwriter Pauline Munier to create this unique film, mixing science fiction, adventure and drama. With talented actors such as Romain Duris, Adèle Exarchopoulos and Nathalie Richard, Animal Kingdom promises to be a captivating cinematic experience.

The screening of Animal Kingdom as the opening film of the Un Certain Regard selection is a testament to the importance of this film at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival. Audiences are eager to discover this original work that explores the consequences of the mutation of man to animal and how society adapts to this new reality.

Scheduled to be released on October 4, 2023, Animal Kingdom is set to be an eventful film that will captivate audiences and make its mark at this year's Cannes Film Festival. The combination of an intriguing script, a careful direction and a renowned cast should ensure the success of this opening film of the Un Certain Regard selection.

In short, Thomas Cailley's Animal Kingdom is a film not to be missed at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival. As the opening film of the Un Certain Regard selection, this work promises to immerse viewers in a world where man and animal become one, with talented actors such as Romain Duris and Adèle Exarchopoulos in the cast.
